Our Story

The Begining

Founded in 2020, Bubble Recap brings authentic Taiwanese flavors to your doorstep. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create an extraordinary dining experience that combines traditional Taiwanese cuisine with modern culinary artistry.

We take pride in importing premium ingredients directly from Taiwan, ensuring every dish maintains its authentic taste and quality. From our signature bubble teas to our innovative fusion dishes, we carefully select each ingredient to deliver an unforgettable dining experience. Our expert chefs, trained in traditional Taiwanese cooking techniques, craft each dish with precision and passion.

At Bubble Recap, we believe in serving more than just food - we create memories. Our commitment to excellence extends from our kitchen to your table, making us a preferred destination for those seeking authentic Taiwanese flavors and exceptional service.
